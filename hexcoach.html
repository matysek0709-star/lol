How this works

What it does: every interval, it grabs one snapshot of your shared screen and asks Claude to look at it as a League coach, then shows (and optionally speaks) one short tip.

What it can't do: it isn't reading game memory or live combat logs, so it can't react instantly mid-fight — it's a periodic glance, like a coach looking over your shoulder every 10-60 seconds. It also can't hear your game audio.

Best setup: run this in a desktop browser (Chrome or Edge) on your gaming PC, launch League in windowed or borderless mode, then share that window (not the whole desktop) so the analysis stays focused on the game.

Privacy: screenshots are sent to Claude for analysis and are not stored by this page. Closing the tab stops everything immediately.
